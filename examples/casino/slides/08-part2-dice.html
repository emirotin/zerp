Two dice: what is the probability of a sum of 7?

Total combinations: 6 × 6 = 36. How many give a sum of 7?

Press ↓ to highlight combinations that sum to 7

P(sum = 7) = 6/36 = 1/6 ≈ 16.7%

Seven is the most common sum. But two (1+1) is only 1/36 ≈ 2.8%
