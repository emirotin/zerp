Expected value

A $100 bet on red. What happens on average?

P(red) = 18/37
P(not red) = 19/37
E = (18/37) × (+100) + (19/37) × (−100)
E = (1800 − 1900) / 37
E = −100/37 ≈ −$2.70

On average, you lose $2.70 on every $100 bet

House edge = 1/37 ≈ 2.7%
