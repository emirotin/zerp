Joseph Jagger: an engineer and a biased wheel

1873
Monte Carlo

An English engineer hired 6 assistants.

Task: for one week, write down every number that came up.

Result: notebooks filled with thousands of numbers.

He discovered that on one wheel, certain numbers came up noticeably more often. A mechanical defect.

Winnings: millions

in today's money

The casino panicked, moved the wheels around, and then replaced them entirely.
